# Laser 247 Play - Brand Style Guide

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Laser 247 Play  
**Domain:** laser247play.com  
**Tagline:** *Free-to-Play Cricket Entertainment & Fun*  
**Positioning:** Educational cricket prediction and team-building platform for entertainment purposes

---

## Brand Identity

### Mission
To provide a free, fun, and educational cricket entertainment platform where users can test their cricket knowledge, build teams, and compete on leaderboards without any financial pressure.

### Key Values
- **Free-to-Play** - No money involved, completely accessible
- **Educational** - Learn cricket strategy and team composition
- **Entertainment** - Fun and engaging cricket experience
- **Fair Play** - Transparent, skill-based competition
- **Responsible** - 18+ only, compliant with regulations

---

## Typography

### Headings
- **Font Family:** Inter, Roboto, or similar modern sans-serif
- **Weight:** Bold (700) for H1-H2, Semi-bold (600) for H3-H4
- **Style:** Clean, geometric, highly legible

### Body Text
- **Font Family:** Inter, Roboto, or similar modern sans-serif
- **Weight:** Regular (400) for body, Medium (500) for emphasis
- **Line Height:** 1.6 for optimal readability

## Logo Usage

### Main Logo
- File: `logo_main.png`
- Features "LASER 247 PLAY" with integrated cricket elements
- Minimum size: 120px width for digital use
- Clear space: Minimum 20px padding on all sides

### Logo Variations
- **Light Background:** Use full-color logo
- **Dark Background:** Use white or light version
- **Small Sizes:** Use simplified icon version if needed

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch or distort proportions
- Do not change colors
- Do not add effects or shadows
- Do not place on busy backgrounds

## Design Principles

### 1. Clean & Modern
- Minimalist layouts with ample white space
- Card-based design for content organization
- Subtle shadows and rounded corners

### 2. Mobile-First
- Responsive design for all screen sizes
- Touch-friendly buttons and interactions
- Optimized performance on mobile devices

### 3. High Contrast
- Ensure WCAG AA accessibility standards
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Readable text on all backgrounds

### 4. Action-Oriented
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Prominent "Register" and "Build Team" buttons
- Visual feedback on interactions

---

## Tone of Voice

### Communication Style
- **Friendly & Approachable** - Welcoming to all cricket fans
- **Educational** - Informative without being condescending
- **Exciting** - Convey the thrill of cricket competition
- **Transparent** - Clear about free-to-play, no-money model
- **Responsible** - Emphasize age restrictions and fair play

### Content Guidelines
- Avoid the word "fantasy" - use "prediction" or "team-building"
- Emphasize "free", "fun", "entertainment", "educational"
- Always mention "no real money" and "18+ only"
- Use cricket terminology appropriately
- Keep language simple and accessible
